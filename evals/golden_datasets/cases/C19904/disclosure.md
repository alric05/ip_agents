# A novel design of floating structure for eco-adaptive floating photovoltaic system: eco-float voltaic platform (EFVP)

## Technical Field

The disclosed technique relates to floating solar panel array installations, in general, and to methods and systems for constructing cost-effective floating solar panel and solar panel array installations with mooring and anchoring, in particular.

## Background / Problem Statement

Smaller countries, such as Lebanon, Israel, Bahrain and Qatar are at a disadvantage in this respect, as undeveloped land may be sparse and scarce and also subject to competition amongst various governmental needs for use of any undeveloped land.
