# Invention Scope

## Customer Idea (Original)
A novel design of floating structure for eco-adaptive floating photovoltaic system: eco-float voltaic platform (EFVP). Floating PV with rectangular float having divided top platform and four walls; cross-shaped support inside to retain four pontoons; bifacial PV panel mounted using two triangular supports; mooring with concrete blocks + plastic-coated stainless-steel wires to corners; sensors (top/bottom panel temperature, irradiance) with data logger and wireless transmission to central database; junction box under panel with current/voltage sensors and telemetry; power cable to load.

## Clarifications / Assumptions (Defaults Confirmed)
- Modular system: a single float module intended to be repeated into an array.
- Pontoons are separate buoyant elements inserted/retained by the internal cross-shaped frame.
- Divided top platform: two deck sections separated by a gap/slot (not necessarily hinged).
- One bifacial PV panel per module (representative claim unit).
- Wireless telemetry is protocol-agnostic (Wi‑Fi/cellular/LoRa etc.) to a central database.

## Confirmed Scope
A modular floating PV platform module comprising: (i) a rectangular outer float/frame with four side walls and a divided top deck; (ii) an internal cross-shaped support that creates four openings and captures four pontoons; (iii) bifacial PV mounting via paired triangular supports on opposite sides of the deck; (iv) a mooring/anchoring approach using concrete blocks and plastic-coated stainless-steel wires attached at float corners; and (v) integrated instrumentation (top/bottom PV temperature, irradiance) and electrical performance monitoring (current/voltage at junction box) with a data logger that wirelessly transmits to a central database.
